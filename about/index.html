I love to create.

I am a full-stack software engineer living in Portland, Oregon. I have been involved in application configuration and development for three years, and am a proponent of using progressive programming methods to build user-focused applications that empower people.

I transitioned to tech from the legal industry, where I was a legal assistant for 9 years. When I chose to embark in a new direction, I knew it would not be easy. Today, my technical skill set consists of fluency in Typescript & JavaScript, React, SQL, PostgreSQL, Express, and Node. I aspire to create technology that is accessible across the socioeconomic spectrum, and that heightens creativity, builds connections, and spurs innovation.

The most interesting and fulfilling project not featured on my technical resume is Le Fog, my musical moniker. Creating music as Le Fog brings me joy and offers a creative outlet that complements the creativity I bring to software development.
